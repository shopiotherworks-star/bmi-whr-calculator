Normal BMI Ranges for Asians

  • Underweight: BMI < 18.5
  • Normal: BMI 18.5 - 22.9
  • Overweight: BMI 23.0 - 27.4
  • Obese: BMI ≥ 27.5

WHR Ranges

  • For Women: WHR ≥ 0.81 (High Health Risk)
